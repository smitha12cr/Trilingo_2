Explore What Makes Us Different

Learning

Mother-Tongue-First Curriculum

Our curriculum is built on the principle that children learn best in the language they speak at home. Every lesson begins in the mother tongue and gradually bridges to other languages.

  • ✓Phonetic-based reading modules
  • ✓Culturally relevant stories and examples
  • ✓Progressive language bridging exercises
  • ✓Age-appropriate content for 5–14 years
Audio

Native Speaker Audio & Pronunciation

Authentic pronunciation matters. Our platform features recordings from native speakers of each tribal language, ensuring children learn correct sounds, tones, and expressions from the start.

  • ✓High-quality native speaker recordings
  • ✓Speech recognition for practice
  • ✓Slow and normal playback speeds
  • ✓Community-contributed audio library
Analytics

Progress Tracking & Insights

Parents, teachers, and community leaders can track learning progress with detailed dashboards. Understand where a child excels and where they need support.

  • ✓Individual learner dashboards
  • ✓Classroom and group analytics
  • ✓Weekly progress reports
  • ✓Skill mastery indicators
Safety

Child-Safe & Privacy-First

We take child safety and data privacy seriously. Our platform is COPPA-compliant, ad-free, and designed with child protection at its core.

  • ✓No advertisements or external links
  • ✓End-to-end data encryption
  • ✓Parental consent controls
  • ✓Zero data sharing with third parties
