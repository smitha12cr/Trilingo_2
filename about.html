About Us

We are on a mission to make education accessible, inclusive, and culturally rooted for every tribal child across India.

Our Story

Where It All Began

Mother Tongue Learning was born from a simple yet powerful observation: millions of tribal children across India struggle in school because they are taught in languages they do not speak at home. This language gap creates confusion, discouragement, and ultimately, higher dropout rates.

We started with a question — what if children could learn in the language they already know? What if education could begin with understanding rather than confusion?

"When a child learns in their mother tongue, they do not just understand words — they understand the world around them." — Our Founding Principle

Today, we are building a digital platform that empowers tribal communities to preserve, teach, and celebrate their languages through interactive, culturally connected education.

Timeline

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped Mother Tongue Learning.

2023

The Beginning

Research and community outreach began across tribal regions in Jharkhand and Odisha to understand language barriers in education.

2024

Platform Development

Started building the digital learning platform with native speakers, educators, and linguists. First lessons created in Mundari and Santali.

2025

Pilot Launch

Launched the first version of the app with interactive lessons, quizzes, and progress tracking for Mundari and Santali learners.

2026

Expanding Horizons

Added Ho language support, partnered with schools and NGOs, and launched the volunteer program for community-driven content creation.

Future

The Road Ahead

Aiming to support 20+ tribal languages, reach 100,000+ learners, and establish Mother Tongue Learning as a national movement for inclusive education.
