FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

Which languages are currently available?

We currently support Mundari, Santali, and Ho. Kurukh, Kharia, Gondi, and Bhil are coming soon. We are continuously adding more languages based on community demand.

Can schools use this for free?

Yes! We offer free access to schools in tribal areas. Contact us through our partnership program to learn more about institutional access and support.

How can I contribute as a volunteer?

You can help with vocabulary creation, translations, audio recordings, lesson design, and community testing. Visit our Volunteer section to get started.

Is the platform accessible offline?

Yes, our Pro Learner and Institution plans include offline mode, allowing learners to download lessons and use them without an internet connection.

What devices are supported?

Our platform works on Android and iOS mobile devices, tablets, and desktop browsers. We optimize for low-end devices and slow internet connections.

How do you ensure cultural accuracy?

All content is created and reviewed by native speakers and cultural experts from each community. We follow a community-driven validation process.
